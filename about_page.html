About VELORE

Our Story

Isabella Rossi

Founder

Isabella Rossi, a visionary in the fragrance industry, founded VELORE with a passion for crafting scents that evoke emotion and tell stories. Her journey began with a deep appreciation for the art of perfumery, inspired by her travels and the rich tapestry of cultures she encountered.

Our Mission

At VELORE, our mission is to create exceptional fragrances that capture the essence of luxury and sophistication. We are committed to using the finest ingredients and sustainable practices to deliver an unparalleled olfactory experience.

Our Journey

2018: The Beginning

VELORE is founded by Isabella Rossi, driven by a vision to redefine luxury perfumery.

2020: First Collection

The debut collection, 'Essence of Dreams,' is launched, receiving critical acclaim for its unique blends.

2023: Global Recognition

VELORE expands its presence globally, earning accolades for its commitment to quality and innovation.
